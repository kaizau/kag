Travel Information

Last updated: May 14, 2022


So you're thinking about attending in person? Awesome.


Taiwan's entry policies are challenging but not impossible. Restrictions have been relaxed in recent weeks, and there's a good chance they'll be even easier by May.


Taiwan currently requires a visa, 7-day quarantine, and negative PCR tests upon arrival.


Stay tuned, and contact Kai if you have any questions!

Requirements

  • Travel visa (we can potentially help with this)
  • PCR test 2 days before flight
  • PCR test on arrival
  • Quarantine for 7 days (hotel or household with sufficient privacy)
  • At-home rapid antigen tests on days three, five, and seven of quarantine
  • PCR test on day 7
